Who Am I?

My name is Tony. I am a 20-something year old autistic developer, video editor and overall geek from the New England region of the United States.

From a young age, I had a profound interest in technology. Just about every scrap piece of electronic I could get my little hands on was dismantled, studied and theorized until understanding it. Once the internet got involved, my interest in technology only grew further with PC gaming, Adobe Photoshop and delving deep into programs like Registry Editor.

I also enjoy building my own rigs. I upgraded my very desktop at the age of seven years old, upgrading my hand-me-down Dell Windows Vista into a mid-tier gaming PC. Nowadays, I build all my computers from scratch. I believe modern "PC gaming culture" has trivialized the raw enjoyment of building computers for many and follow use-based priorities as a huge "fuck you" to spec-shaming, brand indoctrination, etc.

I am also an amateur programmer. I was tutored by my friend Chris Video Greek, and can now write decently in both Python and Javascript. I plan to Assembly soon.
